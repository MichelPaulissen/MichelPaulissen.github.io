Space Tramp Brains Eden Games Jam 2012

Space Tramp is the winning game of the Brains Eden Games Jam 2012 at Cambridge.

The game features a character jumping on a trampoline. However, it's not a normal trampoline, but one with which you can reach space! Jump higher and higher, until you are so far into outer space that Earth is but a tiny dot on your screen.

Control the character using the analog stick on your gamepad or the arrow keys on the keyboard. The game becomes more difficult as you get higher into space, as wind (and solar wind) gets stronger. Get higher to earn a new high score.

This was the first time we as a team used Unity3D. We started off a bit slow because we were not used to the art pipeline that you have to invent when you're without the Unity Asset Server. On top of that, we had to think of ways of solving inevitable scaling issues and figure out how to use some of the more obscure features of Unity. As I at the time was the most experienced with the software I was also busy teaching the software to everybody else.

Design: Alex Camilleri
Programming: Mark Scheurwater, Michel Paulissen
Art: Nils Ruisch, Robert van Duursen
Music: Alex Baldwin, Rob Heslop, Ben Hauke
